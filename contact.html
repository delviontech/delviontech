FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the strategy call really free? +
Yes, 100% free with zero obligation. We spend 20 minutes understanding your business and share a clear digital growth plan — no strings attached.
How long before I start seeing results? +
Most clients start receiving leads within 3–7 days of campaign launch. The first week is about testing and optimization to bring your cost per lead down.
What is the minimum budget for running ads? +
We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹10,000–₹15,000/month for meaningful results. Our management fee is separate. We'll suggest the ideal budget for your goals on the strategy call.
Do you offer guaranteed results? +
While no ethical agency can guarantee exact numbers (as results depend on budget, market, and product), we have a strong track record of 50+ leads in 7 days for most clients. We share case studies transparently.
Is there a long-term contract? +
No lock-in contracts. We work month-to-month. We believe if we deliver results, you'll stay. And if you're not happy, you should be free to leave. Simple.
Which cities do you serve? +
We serve businesses across all of India — Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Chennai, Ahmedabad, and every other city. Digital marketing is location-agnostic.
How do we communicate and get reports? +
Primarily via WhatsApp — because that's how India works. You get weekly performance reports, and your dedicated account manager is available on WhatsApp during business hours.
